Welcome we’re glad you’re here.


Jesus was a simple man with a simple plan: to make God known. Guided by His example, we strive to live with purpose, rooted in God’s Word, and committed to becoming the people God has destined us to be. Our mission is to equip young individuals in true Christian doctrine, shaping them to be useful to their local Church and community. We aim to build a fellowship rooted in faith, fostering deep spiritual friendships, and living out God’s love by serving others and making a lasting impact.


who we are


Our Motto

The Motto of the Fellowship shall be “Towards the Perfect Man” (Ephesians 4:13A).

Our Aim

Empowering members with a deep understanding of Christian doctrine to live as true followers of Christ.

Our Objective

Fostering spiritual growth, obedience, and leadership through education, discipleship, and community engagement.
