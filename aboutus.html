About AYF Calabar

Our aim is to train and develop individual members in the true doctrine of Christianity with a view of attaining the standard required of a true follower of Christ by making themselves useful to only to the Church and Community in which they live, but also to the Federal Republic of Nigeria, by respecting constituted Authorities, so that they may always in their daily lives strive “Towards the Perfect Man”…..




aim & objective

To train and develop Youths in the doctrine of Christianity towards attainment of the standard required of a true follower of Christ by making themselves useful to the church, the Nation and the Commuunity, striving daily to emulate the ideals of the perfect Man (Jesus)

Together, we constantly look at Jesus through God’s Word, inspired to become whatever destiny requires so that we can give hope to the many still unfamiliar with God’s amazing love.
